The highly developed field of urology treats a variety of genitourinary diseases, including kidney stones, enlarged prostates, Cancer of kidney, Prostate Cancer, Bladder Cancer and Testicular Diseases, etc
The field of medicine known as urology, commonly referred to as genitourinary surgery, focuses on illnesses of the male and female urinary tracts as well as the male reproductive systems. The kidneys, adrenal glands, ureters, urine bladder, urethra, and male reproductive organs are among the organs that fall under the purview of urology.
At our hospital in Kharghar, you can receive top-notch care at a reasonable price thanks to our urology-trained staff and cutting-edge technologies. The entire endoscopic, laparoscopic and robotic treatment of urological problems such as stones, enlarged prostates, etc. is our area of expertise. Additionally, we have a wealth of experience treating people with sexual dysfunction and infertility in men. Additionally, our team is prepared to handle urological malignancies such bladder, prostate,  and kidney cancer treatment in Kharghar. Trust us for your urological needs – your dedicated Urologist in Kharghar.

01. Kidney and Ureteral Stones

This is a condition in which a solid material is formed due to the presence of certain substances in higher proportion in urine. These can be extremely painful and can be found blocking the urine flow from kidneys to the urinary bladder. They are of various sizes and shapes; hence treatment is based on the aspects like- location of the stone, size, surface of stone and shape of stone.Bladder stones.

05.Neurogenic Bladder:

The urinary bladder has nerves and muscles that work in synchronisation to allow urination to occur. But in cases of nerve injury due to an illness or trauma, the nerve endings to the bladder are damaged as well which hamper normal urination. Due to this miscommunication between the nerves and muscles of the bladder occur resulting in improper emptying of the bladder.
