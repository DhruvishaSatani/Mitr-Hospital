Accident & Emergency
Department

The Emergency Department, often referred to as the ER or ED, is a critical component of any healthcare facility. It serves as the primary point of access for patients seeking immediate medical attention for a wide range of illnesses and injuries. The ED is designed to provide rapid assessment, stabilization, and treatment to patients facing life-threatening conditions or situations requiring urgent medical intervention.
